### Delta Signal Scale

```
WATCH LIST: [Top 3-4 indicators for next cycle]
```

### Standing Rules (Apply Every Cycle)

1. Always compare to the most recent scored cycle — not the most recent calendar date.
2. Only count confirmed data. Estimates flagged with (est.).
3. Data >30 days old flagged as [STALE].
4. **Geopolitical Override:** Active kinetic conflict = L2 forced to score 1.0.
5. Minimum delta for posture change = plus/minus 0.25 composite.
6. No backfill: untracked prior indicators marked [NEW — no prior baseline].

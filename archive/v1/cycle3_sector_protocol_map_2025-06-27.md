## 4. AI AGENTS

### Why AI Agents Win
- Largest technology narrative of this era meets crypto native properties (wallets, payments, trustless coordination)
- Autonomous agents that transact need blockchains. Blockchains need agents as users.
- Flywheel: more agents = more on-chain activity = more protocol revenue = more capital for agents

### Critical Warning
"99% of AI agents will disappear." (Bitget Research, 2025)

This is a Cambrian explosion. Most will die. Value concentrates in:
1. Infrastructure protocols (not individual agents)
2. Platforms with genuine network effects
3. Agents with verifiable, recurring revenue

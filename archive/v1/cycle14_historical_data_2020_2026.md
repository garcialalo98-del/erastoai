## Key Patterns for Backtesting

1. **L1 leads price 1-2 quarters.** L1 peaked 2020-21 → BTC ATH Nov 21. L1 crashed Q1 22 → BTC bottom 8mo later.
2. **Score ≥3.5 = profitable next Q** (8/9 times). Validates allocation threshold.
3. **Score <2.0 = capital destruction.** Every sub-2.0 Q preceded 20-60% drawdowns.
4. **Fastest drops = shocks.** 2022 Q1 (-0.97), 2025Q4→2026Q1 (-0.67). Emergency rebalancing triggers.
